## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Solutions Architect** and **Full-Stack Engineer** with over a decade of experience designing and delivering cloud-native platforms that hold up under real production load. From early-stage SaaS products to enterprise-grade **FinTech**, **e-commerce**, **healthcare**, and **AI-powered** systems, I've built architectures for product teams across the **USA**, **UAE**, **Oman**, **Saudi Arabia**, **Ireland**, and **Malaysia**, leading distributed engineering teams of up to **20 people** while staying hands-on when it matters.

Most engineering problems that look like technology problems are architecture problems in disguise — performance bottlenecks, tangled codebases, and backends that work fine in demos but fail at scale. I diagnose the root cause and build foundations where **growth becomes a feature, not a liability**.

Currently at **Noorcom**, I focus on platform stability, backend performance, and CI/CD-driven delivery. Previously, I architected LLM pipelines, microservices, and multi-tenant SaaS platforms for product teams across the **GCC** and **United States**.

> 📍 **Open to:** Solutions Architect, Technical Architect, and Engineering Leadership roles, with a strong focus on the **GCC region** (Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the wider Gulf market).
